About

Based between city and coastline, working mostly in portraiture and observed environments.

Images are sequenced like essays: opener, pause, turn, release. The site follows the same logic by keeping text brief and allowing the spacing between blocks to do some of the editorial work.

Method

Digital and film capture, light retouching, sequence-first editing.

Exhibitions

Independent zines, small gallery walls, editorial commissions, collaborative projects.

Formats

Editorial portraits, travel essays, seasonal journals, monochrome studies.
